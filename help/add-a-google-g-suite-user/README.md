# Add a Google G Suite User

![g_suite_logo_1](g_suite_logo_1.png)

This post shows how to add a user to your Google G Suite.

**Steps**

1\. Sign-in to [https://admin.google.com/](http://admin.google.com/)

2\. Click **Users**

![click_users_2](click_users_2.png)

3\. Put your mouse over the **circle** with a **+**

**Note:** depending on the size of your window, the '+' will be in the upper left corner or lower right corner.

![hover_cursor_over_circle_with_plus_3](hover_cursor_over_circle_with_plus_3.png)

You should see:

![add_new_user_4](add_new_user_4.png)

4\. **Click** on the '**+'**

![click_add_new_user_5](click_add_new_user_5.png)

You should see the **Add new user screen**:

![add_new_user_screen_6](add_new_user_screen_6.png)

5\. Fill in the new user's details and click **ADD NEW USER**

![add_new_user_7](add_new_user_7.png)

6\. Click **DONE**

![click_done_8](click_done_8.png)
